Lore and Storyline

In Valorant lore, Jett is an agile agent expert in the art of evasion. She overwhelms enemies and wounds them with a blade without them knowing her position. Of Korean origin, Jett is an agile fighter who gives priority to movement. Her abilities focus on stealth with jumps that allow her to reach high ledges, teleportation, and smoke bombs.
